Simple, Seamless, Effective

Four steps. Zero headaches. Real results.

01

Visitor Hits Play

The viewer presses play on your embedded YouTube video. The watch-time counter begins only when the video is actually playing — not during buffering or pauses.

02

6-Second Trigger

Once exactly 6 seconds of genuine watch time is accumulated, the video automatically pauses. The page blurs and a polished modal slides into view.

03

Lead Captured

The visitor fills in their name, email, and mobile number. All fields are validated. A success screen confirms registration instantly.

04

Video Resumes

One click on "Return to Video" closes the modal, removes the blur, and resumes playback from exactly where it was paused. The experience feels natural.
